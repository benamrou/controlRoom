Use this functionnality on business request. The action will stop and restart the warehouse services. On-going operations will be disturbed.

Make sure warehouse is aware about the warehouse processes restart.
The following warehouse services are going to be restarted:
  • GOLD Vocal (VDW) : this service is used for pickers to prepare and load the pallet using their Vocal devices.
  • Warehouse printers (Samba): this service is used during reception and preparation to print logistics document.
  • GOLD Radio : this service is used during reception and internal pallet movement by the fork lift.
  • GOLD Stock : this service is used by all the warehouse associates.
  • GOLD GWVO : this service is used by prep. managers and leads to track the workload and define voice devices for picking/preparation.

The restart process is usually taking between 5 and 7 minutes.
